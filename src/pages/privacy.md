_Last updated_: 3 June 2026

This page describes how the **DHInfra.at** website (this public, informational site) handles your
data. The short version: this website does not collect personal data about you.

## What we collect

**Nothing, by design.** This is a static website. It does not use tracking cookies, advertising,
user accounts, or analytics that identify you. We do not build profiles of visitors and we do not
sell or share any visitor data — because we do not have any.

If we later add privacy-friendly, aggregate usage statistics (for example a cookieless analytics
tool that records only anonymous totals such as page views, with no personal data), we will update
this page first.

## Server logs

Like any website, the server that delivers these pages may automatically process technical data —
such as your IP address, browser type, and the page requested — for the purpose of delivering the
site and keeping it secure. This is standard for web hosting, is not used to identify you, and is
handled by our hosting provider on our behalf.

## Links to other services

This site links out to third-party services (for example video conferencing, software repositories,
and partner institutions). When you follow such a link, that service's own privacy policy applies.
We have no control over, and take no responsibility for, the data practices of external sites.

## The DHInfra.at platform is separate

This website is **not** the operational DHInfra.at platform (the compute cluster, login, and
console). Access to the platform requires federated authentication and is governed by its own
[Privacy Notice for Federated Login](/privacy-notice), which describes how personal data is processed
when you sign in via ACOnet/eduID or eduGAIN.

## Your rights and contact

If you have any question about data protection in connection with this website, contact us at
[dhinfra@uni-graz.at](mailto:dhinfra@uni-graz.at). The responsible party and full contact details
are listed in the [Imprint](/imprint).
